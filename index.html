Protection & Event Analysis

DMS Topology & Reliability

02 — About

Where grid operations meet aerospace systems engineering

I'm an Electrical Engineer working as a Network Operations Analyst at an electric distribution utility, where I analyze protection settings and fault events, build Python tooling for distribution-network topology, and calculate reliability indicators (SAIDI/SAIFI) under IEEE Std 1366.

My research background is in systems engineering for aerospace: since 2022 I've contributed to high-altitude balloon (HAB) missions at Observatorio Micro-Macro, designing power subsystems and communications links for near-space environments — a methodology I also applied to my undergraduate thesis under Dr. Jesús González-Llorente (Space Systems Engineering, Senior Member IEEE). I also teach electrical engineering at the university level and mentor undergraduate research.
